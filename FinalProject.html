Final Project

In this game the goal is to dodge the barrels that are falling from the sky while also collecting the coins that fall.
For every coin you collect you earn 5 points, but every time yo get hit by a barrel you lose 1 life and 10 points.
You start out with three lives and you can collect extra lives, but they fall at an decreasing rate as the game goes on.
When you run out of lives the game will end and you have the option of retrying or returning to the main menu.

Another additional feature to our game is the customization area. With this you can select the shirt, pants, and hair color
of the main character in the game.
