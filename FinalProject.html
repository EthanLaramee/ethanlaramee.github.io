Final Project

In this game, the goal is to dodge the barrels that are falling from the sky while also collecting the coins that fall.
For every coin you collect you will earn 5 points, but every time you get hit by a barrel you will lose 10 points and 1 life.
You start out with three lives and you can collect extra lives throughout the game, but they fall at an decreasing rate as the game goes on.
The game contains levels, based on your current score, where the speed will increase the higher your score.
To move you use the arrow keys, and if you would like to sprint press the "SHIFT" key. Sprinting does not last forever, you can only
hold it down for 5 seconds at a time. When you run out of lives the game will end, you will be shown your final score, and you have
the option of replaying or returning to the main menu.

Another additional feature to our game is the customization area. With this you can select the shirt, pants, and hair color
of the main character in the game.
